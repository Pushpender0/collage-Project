Welcome to AIRBUS

It is a web-based platform for airline operating companies as well as their customer. The main aim of this project is to help the airline companies to manage their customers, booking and operations properly. It makes all operations of the airline company easy and accurate. The online platform makes booking management easy by handling customer requests and providing services for the customers located at different parts of the globe. Even This online platform is also helpful to user point of view. User can easily search many option on this web portal. They can search flight, find their address and contact no, book it and do many more as per their requirement. Different modules have been incorporated in this project to handle different parts and sectors of the airline management field.
